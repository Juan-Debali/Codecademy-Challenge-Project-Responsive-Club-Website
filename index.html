ABOUT

The Debali's Sailing Boats was founded in 2023. Located in Florianópolis (Brazil), this club brings together practitioners and fans of sailing boats. It currently has 67 members. The club aims to promote sailing classes, panoramic tours and championships. Come sail with us!
